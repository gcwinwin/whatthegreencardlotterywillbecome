Diversity Visa Lottery — Interactive Application Portal

Pursuant to Section 203(c) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (INA), the United States makes up to 55,000 immigrant visas available annually through a randomized selection process. Effective FY-2026, applicants must complete the Digital Randomization Experience (DRE) module below.

Step-by-Step Application Instructions
  1. 1
    Upload your passport — Provide a scan of your valid biometric passport. Accepted formats: PDF, JPG, PNG. Your data is encrypted using Military-Grade Security™ and transmitted to a government cloud server located somewhere secure.
  2. 2
    Pay the processing convenience fee $1.00 USD — Per Executive Directive 9981-B, a non-refundable administrative modernization fee is required to access the Digital Randomization Experience. Fees are $1 for the first spin, then $100, $5,000, $20,000, and $100,000 per spin thereafter. This fee funds important government things.
  3. 3
    Spin the Wheel of Opportunity — The randomized selection algorithm, certified by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), will determine your eligibility. Results are legally binding. The wheel does not lie.
  4. 4
    Receive your determination — Winners will be contacted via certified mail within 6–8 business years. Losers are encouraged to try again. Each subsequent spin costs more. This is by design.
